Which wine people most like?

Here is some data from WineEnthusiast. Is recorded a total of 147854 data.

Choosing the right wine is not an easy task for most people. People usually buy wine by asking the shop assistant, but because of the different taste between the shop assistant and themself, most times people does not buy the right one. Hopefully the following visualization of information will help you solve this problem.

This data is get from Twitter, people will write down all information for their favorite wine.

This data are include from 48 countries people who are WineEnthusiasts.

Both chart shows the relationship between points and price, top one is only show US data, bottom one show others. As you can see, US people like to buy wines under $400.

This chart is shows the relationship between country and price. As you can see, Many wines in France, Germany, Italy, Portugal, Spain and US cost more than $200. This also shows that people in these countries prefer wine more than other countries.

The chart below shows the relationship between what points people give for each wine and the price of wine. The points WineEnthusiasts give is between 80 to 100. They also give the wine's price, the funny thing is some wine have different price. As a whole, it shows an upward trend. The most point people score is 88.
